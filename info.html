History of Fortia

The Kingdom of Fortia was founded upon the principles of Self-Fortism. Fortia was established as a sovereign micronation focused on freedom, responsibility and progress. The nation began with the vision of creating a structured society where citizens could contribute, create and build a future together.

Government

Fortia operates under a constitutional structure guided by its doctrine and founding principles. The government exists to protect citizens, maintain order and support national growth.

Currency

The official currency of Fortia is the Fortian Valor (FVR). Fortian Valor represents strength, stability and value within the Kingdom of Fortia.

National Information

National Colors: Red • White • Gold
