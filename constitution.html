Constitution of The Kingdom Of Fortia

Preamble

We, the citizens and founders of Fortia, establish this Constitution to create a nation based upon Self-Fortism, justice, unity, responsibility and progress.

Article I — Sovereignty

Fortia exists as a sovereign micronation guided by its Constitution and national values.

Article II — Citizenship

Citizens may apply for Fortian citizenship and shall receive rights and protections under Fortian law.

Article III — Rights

All Fortian citizens have rights including:

• Freedom of expression
• Equal treatment under law
• Privacy
• Due process

Article IV — Currency

Official Currency: Fortian Valor (FVR)

Article V — National Symbols

Golden Eagle
